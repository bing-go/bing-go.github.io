About Us:
We are just a group of students that decided to shift some of our time away from school work in order to chase after our dream of starting a business. We came up with the idea for Bing Go while studying in the library one finals week. Our vision is to allow students to one day get any type of food on campus without having to go through the trouble of driving out and back.
